Welcome to Food Galore!

France is one of the best countries when it comes to food. It has so many varieties of foods and is such a great place to try new things at.

Some of the national dishes are Basil salmon terrine, Pot-au-feu, Crème brûlée and Crêpes

Food by each region:

Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur

The most famous dishes from this region of France are Ratatouille, Pan bagnat,  Daube and Bourride

Toulouse

The most famous dishes from this region of France are Cassoulet, Aligot,  Croustade and Roquefort

Bordeaux

The most famous dishes from this region of France are Confit de Canard,  Black Périgord Truffle and Tourin

                  Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes

The most famous dishes from this region of France are Quiche, Gratin Dauphinois and Condrieu Wine
